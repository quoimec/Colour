WHAT ARE COLOURS?
(🇺🇸 see: colors)
So what exactly are colours?

How does your screen make this pink, this yellow, and this orange?
The easiest way to understand is to think of your screen as a painter 👩‍🎨 that only has three colours of paint to work with: red, green, and blue.

Just like with paint, your screen can combine these three colours to make new colours. For example, when you mix red with blue, you get purple!
If you come and look really, really close, you'll see that your screen is actually made of up of millions of tiny little dots. Each of these dots is called a pixel.

Inside each pixel is three tiny lights, one red, one green, and one blue.

To make any colour, your screen will turn these lights on and off, mixing the colours of the light, just like you do with paint.
